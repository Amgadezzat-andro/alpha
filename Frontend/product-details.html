Self-Adhesive Labels: Features and Uses

We have more than 30 years of experience in the printing industry, which allows us to offer you the most comprehensive range of personalized self-adhesive labels. We've always appreciated a challenge, and over time we have developed creative solutions that can cover all market needs.

Today, at Alpha Pack, we produce everything from the most simple custom labels to the most complex, in distinctive shapes, textures and colors.

Self-adhesive labels are the Star

Self-adhesive labels are the star of our portfolio and probably the most versatile product used in packaging. Whether your product packaging is made of glass, foil, plastic, metal or wood, we print personalized adhesive labels that are easy to apply without using heat, solvent or water.
