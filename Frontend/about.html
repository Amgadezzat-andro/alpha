About Alpha Pack

Over 20 years of mastering the art of creating high-quality adhesive labels that help brands communicate, stand out, and build trust.

Who We Are

With over 20 years in the business, we've mastered the art of creating high-quality adhesive labels that do more than stick. They help brands communicate, stand out, and build trust at first glance.

We specialize in adhesive labels for food, retail, pharmaceutical, and industrial applications, delivering eye-catching shelf appeal, reliable adhesion, and long-lasting durability in every piece we produce.

From self-adhesive labels and barcode labels to shrink sleeves, stretch sleeves, tamper-proof labels, and in-mold labels — Alpha Pack offers a complete range of labeling solutions to meet every packaging need.

Our Story

Our History

From humble beginnings to industry leadership — our journey of innovation and quality.

Early 2000s — Foundation

Alpha Pack was founded with a commitment to producing high-quality adhesive labels. Starting with a small team and a single press, we set out to serve local businesses with reliable label solutions.

2005–2010 — Growth & Expansion

As demand grew, we expanded our production capabilities and invested in advanced flexographic printing technology, allowing us to serve larger clients across food, retail, and pharmaceutical sectors.

2010–2015 — Diversification

We broadened our product range to include shrink sleeves, stretch sleeves, barcode labels, and tamper-proof labels, becoming a one-stop shop for packaging and labeling solutions.

2015–2020 — Innovation

Investment in in-mold labeling (IML) technology and premium materials like clear-on-clear and aluminum labels positioned us as a leader in high-end packaging solutions.

2020–Present — Industry Leadership

Today, with over 20 years of expertise, 500+ satisfied clients, and millions of labels produced, Alpha Pack continues to set the standard for quality, innovation, and customer service in the labeling industry.
